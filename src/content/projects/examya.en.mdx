Complete conversational bot system for medical laboratory exams. The user chats with **Shuri** on WhatsApp, searches for exams by name, symptom or nearby lab suggestions, generates orders automatically and coordinates payments.

## Main features

- **Smart search**: Shuri understands natural language queries like "what exams do I need for a general checkup?" or "thyroid exams".
- **Price comparison**: Shows prices from different labs so the user can choose the best option.
- **Order generation**: Automatically creates purchase orders with all the details of the selected exam.
- **Payment coordination**: Integrates payment flows directly via WhatsApp.

## Tech stack

Built with **Next.js** + **TypeScript** on the backend, **Drizzle ORM** with **PostgreSQL** for persistence, and the **Anthropic Claude API** as the conversational AI engine. WhatsApp integration is handled through the WhatsApp Business API.

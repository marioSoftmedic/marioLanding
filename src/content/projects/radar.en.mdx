**RadarPyme** centralizes discovery, monitoring and prioritization of opportunities in **Mercado Público** using AI agents, automation and operational analysis.

The service turns manual tender review into an assisted workflow: it identifies relevant opportunities, detects important changes, summarizes long documents and delivers actionable alerts so teams can decide faster.

## Main features

- **Smart tender discovery**: filters by category, buyer, dates, amounts, keywords and commercial criteria.
- **Continuous monitoring**: tracks new opportunities, modifications, added documents, critical dates and upcoming deadlines.
- **Executive summaries**: agents read tender documents and extract requirements, risks, deadlines, required files and decision points.
- **Actionable alerts**: prioritized notifications with enough context to decide whether an opportunity deserves deeper analysis.
- **Opportunity pipeline**: organizes each tender status to separate discarded, under-review and candidate opportunities.

## Associated services

RadarPyme can support commercial scouting, market surveillance, preliminary tender analysis and a prioritized opportunity agenda for teams selling to the public sector.

## Tech stack

RadarPyme combines **TypeScript**, data automation and AI agents to transform public Mercado Público information into useful operational signals.

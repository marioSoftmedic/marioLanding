**Radar** is a work-in-progress app for detecting, monitoring and prioritizing opportunities in **Mercado Público** using AI agents and automation.

The goal is to turn manual tender discovery into an assisted workflow: find relevant opportunities, track important changes, summarize background documents and trigger alerts when an opportunity deserves human review.

## What we are building

- **Opportunity discovery**: search and filtering for relevant tenders based on commercial and technical criteria.
- **Continuous monitoring**: tracking changes, critical dates and new documents published in Mercado Público.
- **Actionable alerts**: notifications with enough context to decide whether to investigate further.
- **AI assistance**: agents that summarize tender documents, extract requirements and help prioritize opportunities.

## Tech stack

Radar combines **TypeScript**, data automation and AI agents to transform public procurement information into useful operational signals. The app is under active development and is part of applied work on autonomous systems for real-world processes.

Agentic cabin reservation bot in Puerto Natales, Chilean Patagonia. The bot is called **Huillín** — warm, friendly and deeply knowledgeable about the region.

## Main features

- **Agentic engine with Claude API**: Huillín handles complex reservation conversations naturally, understanding dates, preferences and queries about the local area.
- **Bidirectional calendar sync**: iCal integration to sync availability with Airbnb and Booking.com in real time.
- **Integrated payments**: Generates payment links via WhatsApp using **Mercado Pago** and **Stripe**.
- **Complete flow**: From availability check to reservation confirmation and payment — all through WhatsApp.

## Context

Puerto Natales is the gateway to Torres del Paine. The bot is designed to handle the area's high tourist demand, offering a smooth booking experience in both Spanish and English.

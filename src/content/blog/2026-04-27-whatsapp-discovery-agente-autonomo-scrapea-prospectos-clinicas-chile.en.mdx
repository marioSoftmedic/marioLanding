## The Problem

Chile has over 200 clinical laboratories. No centralized system has their WhatsApp numbers. Companies need to contact them to offer medical services. Getting this data manually takes weeks.

## What I Built

WhatsApp Discovery is an autonomous agent that scrapes WhatsApp numbers from clinical laboratories across Chile. Rotating strategy system, automatic reporting, and centralized database.

The system runs every 4 hours as a cron job. Processes 10 laboratories per round. Has completed 141 rounds processing over 1,400 prospects.

## How It Works

**Rotating strategies:**
- Google Business Profile API with bypass techniques
- Advanced web scraping + social media discovery
- Manual Instagram and professional profile searching
- Semantic analysis of laboratory websites

**Automated pipeline:**
1. db_monitor PostgreSQL lists pending prospects
2. Strategy rotation based on HEARTBEAT.md configuration
3. Processing with anti-bot techniques and +569 format validation
4. Insertion into prospects table with detailed statuses
5. Automatic reporting to Slack thread with statistics

**Status system:**
- `pending`: awaiting processing
- `found`: WhatsApp found and validated
- `not_found`: no WhatsApp or not found
- `error`: processing failed

## What I Learned

**59.6% current coverage.** 146 laboratories with WhatsApp out of 240 total. Not perfect, but it scales.

**Strategy rotation is crucial.** Google Business Profile fails with peripheral laboratories. Social strategies work better in small cities. The mix yields better results.

**Number validation is complex.** Many websites have outdated numbers. The system validates +569 format and filters out landline numbers.

**Dynamic self-correction.** When a strategy fails consecutively, the system automatically rotates to the next. No human intervention needed.

## What's Next

Up next: automated CRM integration. When a prospect has WhatsApp, the system sends welcome message with Examya integration.

Also: engagement analysis. Not just having the number, knowing if they respond and what services they need.

The goal: 80% coverage by end of 2026. Chile has many small laboratories in rural areas. Requires new discovery strategies.
